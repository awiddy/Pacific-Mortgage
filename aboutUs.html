Who We Are

Your residential and investment real estate lending expert team

Pacific Mortgage Co has been founded with the purpose of providing home owners and real estate investors the best possible mortgage loan solutions. We represent dozens of banks and mortgage lenders, allowing us to offer our client hundreds of loan programs and customized solutions to each mortgage loan request.

Often times, offering the best interest rate is all that matters, and at other times, being able to work through complex financial statements to match the client with the right loan program is key. We have the resources, experience a, and the lending relationships to offer you the program that fits your goals best.

Stephan Wiederkehr, President & Mortgage Broker has been a in the mortgage industry for two decades and a licensed Mortgage Broker since 2005. I was born and raised in Switzerland and after obtaining a degree in business, I came to the US not planning to stay for a long time. However, after graduating Summa Cum Laude from the University of California at Berkeley I decided to make my home here. I have lived in Southern California ever since, together with my wife and three children.
I founded Pacific Mortgage Co with the goal in mind to provide both excellence in lending to my clients, and to provide a solid infrastructure and support for other loan officers that allows them to excel as well.
Every loan transaction is as unique as is the financial and credit profile of each individual client. Matching a client’s specific situation and goals with the right loan program is my mission and excellence in execution is my daily goal. We have expanded our lending partner network to over 30 lenders and offer hundreds of loan programs. This allows us to offer every client the broadest selection of solutions to their individual situation.
Although, giving our geographic location, we tend to work with many affluent clients, I put the same effort into working with a first time home buyer as I do working with a seasoned real estate investor.
Rather unique for the mortgage industry, I am an expert in both residential and commercial real estate lending. You can rely on our expertise for an apartment purchase as much as you trust us with a VA, FHA, or Reverse mortgage transaction.
Contact me today. I look forward to hearing from you.
