Who We Are

Your residential and investment real estate lending expert team

Pacific Mortgage Co has been founded with the purpose of providing home owners and real estate investors the best possible mortgage loan solutions. We represent dozens of banks and mortgage lenders, allowing us to offer our client hundreds of loan programs and customized solutions to each mortgage loan request.

We not only offer the most competitive interest rates but also will match our clients with sometimes complex financial profiles and tax return structures with loan programs that allow the borrowers to achieve their goals.

Stephan Wiederkehr, President & Mortgage Broker, has been in the Mortgage Lending Industry for almost two decades. Born and raised in Switzerland, he graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of California at Berkeley.
Stephan understands that every loan transaction is as unique as is the financial and credit profile of each individual client. Matching a client’s specific situation and goals with the right loan program is his mission and excellence in execution is his daily goal.
What Stephan enjoys most is to provide his clients with sound advice and educate them about their mortgage loan options. Stephan loves working as much with first time home buyers as he is working with seasoned real estate investors. Stephan strives to always stay informed about changes in the mortgage industry, to stay ahead of the curve, and to always expand the programs available to his clients. That is why he is one of the few mortgage brokers who specialize in both residential and commercial real estate lending. He understands the intricacies of providing financing for an office or retail property as much as he is seasoned in executing a VA, FHA, or Reverse mortgage transaction.
That is why 99% of his business comes from referrals and repeat clients.
